How it works

EduChaintion compiles to the ERC 721 standard of Ethereum token. Since this is not the real implementation, it runs on Ropsten Testnet and is requires a MetaMask account. For development purposes, you can use a private key to connect via web3.

EduChaintion NFT

EduChaintion in form of ERC 721 are NFTs. They can be minted, burned and transfered. Minted EduChaintion NFTs, are on chain asset that represents a graduate's academic achievements. Ranging from,
  • University Degree
  • Digital Certification
  • Badges and Awards
the value of these NFTs comes from it of academic equivalent, meaning each minted NFT should have similar values to the pursued degree or training cost.

Actors

Academic Institution These are verified institutions, that will have the ability to generate NFT. They can be universities, colleges, government training and online courses. The decision for institutions to also have the ability to burn these NFTs if misconduct occurs.
Graduate Graduates are the users that have been issued a EduChaintion NFT. They have the ability to view and showcase their qualifications, with proof of the NFTs.
